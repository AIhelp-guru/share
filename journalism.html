How to Write a News Story

This workflow is an AI-enabled guide for writing a news story
Reference : Southern Connecticut State University>Journalism

Decide on a Topic

Select a timely, newsworthy event or development to write about. What is your goal in covering this topic? What will readers need to know?

Find Good Sources

Who are the most knowledgeable sources you can interview about this topic? What other sources could provide useful information?

Do Background Research

Review background materials to gain an understanding of the topic before interviewing sources. Look for prior news coverage.

Prepare Interview Questions

Draft a list of questions to ask your sources during interviews.

Conduct Interviews

Schedule and conduct interviews with sources. Take thorough notes. Ask followup questions until you fully understand the story.

Focus the Story

What's the angle or focus for your story? Summarize the main point in one sentence.

Outline the Story

Make an outline with the key elements of your story in logical order.

Write First Draft

Write a rough first draft following your outline.

Revise and Edit

Revise for clarity and concision. Proofread for spelling, grammar, punctuation.

Submit Story

Submit polished story to your editor by the deadline.
