Welcome to My Extension!

My Extension is a simple extension that adds a new button to the Chrome toolbar. When you click the button, it will open a new tab to the Google homepage.

Features

  • Adds a new button to the Chrome toolbar
  • Opens a new tab to the Google homepage when you click the button

How to use

  1. Click the new button in the Chrome toolbar.
  2. A new tab will open to the Google homepage.

FAQ

  • Q: Why did you create this extension?
  • A: I created this extension because I wanted a quick and easy way to open the Google homepage from Chrome.
