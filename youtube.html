YouTube - AI deep dive

This AI-powered chrome extension enhances your YouTube experience.

1. Generate Video Summary

Summarizes key points from the video in text form. Summarize this video: {{paste Youtube link}}

2. Suggest Related Videos

Recommends other relevant videos to watch next. Suggest related videos from this:: {{paste Youtube link}}

3. Suggest a comment

Suggests a comment Suggest a short comment to post for this: {{paste Youtube link}}

4. Answer Questions

Answers questions about video content. My question about this video: : {{paste Youtube link}} {{Enter your question}}

5. FAQ

Convert this into Questions and Answers Convert this into 10 key questions and answers format: {{paste Youtube link}}

6. Explain Concepts

Provides more depth on key ideas and topics in the video. Explain key concepts from this video: : {{paste Youtube link}}

6. Create chapters

Create chapters based on the content Identify the chapters in this video with timestamps : {{paste Youtube link}}
